Every time a user opens QGIS, downloads a plugin, or grabs the latest installer, a carefully maintained web of infrastructure quietly does its job in the background. This talk pulls back the curtain on the systems that power the QGIS ecosystem — the website and download pipeline, the plugin repository, feed analytics, and hub.qgis.org — and shares the human side of keeping these services reliable, secure, and scalable for a globally distributed open-source community.
